Pitch & Hook — Subscription Waiver Benefits
Mr. / Mrs. __________, DStv Subscription Waiver will give your beneficiary up to 2 years' prepaid DStv in the unfortunate event of your passing. We have the following options available:
  • Premium: 1 year at R45/month | 2 years at R85/month | 2 years + 220 GB data at R109/month
  • Compact Plus: 1 year at R29/month | 2 years at R55/month | 2 years + 110 GB data at R85/month
  • Compact: 1 year at R23/month | 2 years at R43/month | 2 years + 110 GB data at R75/month
  • Family: 1 year at R19/month | 2 years at R34/month | 2 years + 25 GB data at R43/month
  • Access: 1 year at R7/month | 2 years at R15/month | 2 years + 25 GB data at R29/month
We will not pay the claim if your death is caused by any deliberate, wrongful action or inaction (including suicide) in the first 12 months, or any act of war, riot, strike, civil disobedience, or any military, naval or police action.
The policy waiting period is 6 months — you will not be able to claim within the first 6 months of activation.
If your claim relates to accidental death there will be no waiting period.
The cooling off period is 31 days after the date of receipt of the policy documents.

Only if YES: When was that policy cancelled?
Only if YES: What was the name of the other insurer?

Pitch & Hook — Loss of Income Benefits
Mr. / Mrs.___________ I've noticed that you don't have Subscription Waiver Loss of Income.
Subscription Waiver Loss of Income will cover your DStv subscription and keep your entire family watching DStv for up to six (6) months, in the unfortunate event that:
  • You lose the ability to earn an income
  • You become temporarily or permanently disabled
  • You are retrenched or dismissed
  • Your business is unable to earn an income
  • You experience a health event affecting your ability to work
Subscription Waiver Pitch
Mr./Ms. Client, for only R45 per month, you can take up our Subscription Waiver Plan/Cover. In the unfortunate event of your passing, your beneficiary will continue to receive DStv for the covered period.
In addition, the Subscription Waiver Plan also includes a lump sum payout (Rxxx) to assist your family during a difficult time.
Loss of Income Pitch
To provide even greater protection, you can also add our Loss of Income Benefit for only R25 per month.
This benefit helps keep your DStv subscription active should you experience a qualifying loss of income due to retrenchment, dismissal, or inability to work.
Value Proposition
For only R70 per month, you can enjoy both benefits, giving you protection whether you are no longer there to support your family, or if you lose your income.
Affordability Handling
If the client raises affordability concerns:
I understand your concern regarding affordability. Based on what we've discussed, we can start with the benefit that best suits your current needs.
If protecting your family is most important, we can start with the Subscription Waiver Benefit at R45 per month.
If your concern is protecting your subscription should you lose your income, we can start with the Loss of Income Benefit at R25 per month.
